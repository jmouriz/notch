Runtime resources for the Notch application.

The universal macOS yt-dlp executable and its license are bundled here to
support YouTube imports without requiring a separate installation.

The arm64 LAME executable and its license are bundled to provide self-contained
MP3 encoding. M4A and WAV exports use the native macOS media frameworks.

FFmpeg is not bundled yet. It will be added with the audio export engine after
its architectures, build options, licenses, and update strategy are fixed.
